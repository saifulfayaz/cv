About Me

I am a final-year Electrical Engineering student at the University of Kashmir, on track to graduate in 2026. My academic journey has been driven by a genuine passion for electrical systems, energy infrastructure, and the engineering principles that power modern society.

Beyond my core programme, I hold an NCVT-certified COPA diploma from ITI Bandipora — providing a practical grounding in computing and IT that complements my engineering background.

Working inside a live utility environment gave me firsthand exposure to large-scale power generation, high-voltage substations, and grid-level operations — translating classroom knowledge into the real complexity of critical infrastructure. I come from Bandipora, a district with remarkable renewable energy potential, and I am determined to contribute to its future.
